Our Mission

TestMasterPro.space was born with a simple yet powerful vision: make high‑quality exam preparation accessible to every student, free of cost. We believe that financial constraints should never limit one's potential to crack competitive exams like SSC, Bank, Railway, CUET, and more.

Our platform offers realistic mock tests, detailed performance analytics, AI‑driven recommendations, and a supportive community — all under one roof.

Meet the Founder

Piyush Kashyap, a passionate developer and education advocate, single‑handedly conceptualised and built TestMasterPro to fill the gap he experienced during his own preparation. Understanding the challenges faced by millions of students, he dedicated himself to creating a seamless, mobile‑friendly platform that works even on slow networks.

Piyush continuously updates the question bank, adds new features, and personally responds to feedback. His goal is to see TestMasterPro become the go‑to destination for every government job aspirant in India.
