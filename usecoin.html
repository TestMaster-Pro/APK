How Redemption Works
💸 How do I get cash for my coins?
Select a cash reward option, enter your UPI Name & UPI ID, and submit. Our admin team will verify and transfer the amount to your UPI account within 24 hours. 1,000 coins = ₹10.
👑 What does premium unlock give me?
Premium unlock gives you access to all paid mock tests, detailed analytics, priority support, and exclusive study material. Usually valid for 30 days after approval.
⏳ How long does approval take?
Most requests are processed within 24 hours. You'll see the status update here. If it's been longer, contact us at testmaster.helpdesk@gmail.com.
🔄 Can I submit multiple requests?
Yes! You can submit as many requests as you want, as long as you have sufficient coins for each. Each request is reviewed independently.
❌ What if my request is rejected?
If rejected, your coins are returned to your balance. You'll see the admin's note explaining the reason. You can always submit again with corrections.
