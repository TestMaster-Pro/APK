Privacy Policy

TestMaster Pro is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Data We Collect: When you register, we collect your name, username, mobile number, email address, educational details, and exam preferences. Your test results, scores, and performance analytics are stored to provide you with personalized insights.

How We Use Your Data: Your data is used solely for providing test services, tracking your progress, generating leaderboards, and sending important account-related communications (welcome emails, payment verifications, password resets). We never sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Storage: All data is securely stored in Google Sheets via Google Apps Script. Access is restricted to the application backend and authorized administrators only.

Your Rights: You can update your profile information at any time. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting our support team.
